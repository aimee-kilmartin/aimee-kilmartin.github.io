Cultural Blog: Emotional Intelligence

Exploration of Emotional Intelligence What is it and why is it important?

What Is Emotional Intelligence?

Emotional intelligence is one's sense of emotion in people - this is both within yourself as well as within others. It is the ability to identify, evaluate, control and express emotions. It also encompasses the sense of empathy, impulse control, and how all of these elements are presented in a persons actions.

Emotional intelligence is made up of these key areas:

  • Self-awareness
  • Self-regulation
  • Motivation
  • Empathy
  • Social skills

Each one of these elements plays a role in our overall emotional intelligence skills.

What Are the Differences Between Emotional Intelligence and Intellectual Intelligence?

There are many differences between Emotional intelligence (or Emotional Quotient/EQ) and Intellectual intelligence (or Intellectual Quotient/IQ). Whilst EQ is the understanding and application of human emotions and social cues, IQ is the ability to think logically, comprehend information and abstract ideas, and take new information and apply this elsewhere.

These qualities affect people in different ways - whilst in a working environment a high EQ has a positive affect on ones teamwork, leadership and collaboration with others, a high IQ alone is more aligned to independent work, and one's own ability to problem-solve and apply knowledge.

High IQ is also more closely linked with logical problem-solving such as maths, whereas high EQ will assist in better self-awareness and wellbeing

Why Is Emotional Intelligence Important?

Emotional intelligence is important because as humans we are social beings, and high EQ helps us understand and work with others. This is vital for everything from maintaining a healthy family unit or household, strong friendships or networks, employment and so on. It also helps with your own wellbeing, in self-awareness and regulating your own emotions. High EQ often correlates to overall positivity and the ability to let go of mistakes made and negative emotions.
